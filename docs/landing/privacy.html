Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

The short version: Your data is encrypted, you own it, and you can delete it anytime. We don't sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Project Nobi ("we", "us", "our") operates the Nori AI Companion mobile application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. What We Collect

  • Account info: Email address and display name when you sign up.
  • Conversations: Messages you send to Nori, including text, voice transcriptions, and image descriptions.
  • Memory data: Facts and relationships Nori extracts from your conversations to provide a personalized experience.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics like feature usage, session length, and crash reports.
  • Device info: Device type, OS version, and app version for compatibility and debugging.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Nori companion experience.
  • To remember your conversations and personal context (the core feature).
  • To send you proactive reminders and follow-ups you've opted into.
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance.
  • To process subscription payments.

4. Encryption & Security

All conversation data is encrypted using AES-128 encryption before storage. Your encryption key is derived from your account and never stored in plaintext on our servers.

  • Data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3.
  • Data at rest is encrypted with AES-128.
  • We use secure key derivation (PBKDF2) for encryption keys.
  • Voice messages are transcribed and the audio is not permanently stored.
  • Images are analyzed for context and the original image is not permanently stored on our servers.

5. Bittensor Network

Nori uses the Bittensor decentralized AI network to process your messages. When you send a message:

  • Your message is sent to miners on the Bittensor network for AI processing.
  • Messages sent to miners do not include your personal identity.
  • Miners see only the conversation context needed to generate a response.
  • No personally identifiable information is shared with the network.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these cases:

  • Service providers: Payment processors (Stripe), cloud infrastructure (encrypted).
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect safety.
  • With your consent: If you explicitly ask us to share something.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
  • Export: Export your conversation history and memory data.
  • Correct: Edit or remove specific memories Nori has stored.
  • Opt out: Disable proactive messages, analytics, or specific features.

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymous analytics: Retained for up to 12 months.

9. Children's Privacy

Nori is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
