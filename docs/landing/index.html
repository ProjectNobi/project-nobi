01 — SEMANTIC MEMORY

A friend who truly understands.

Nori doesn't just keyword-match — she uses semantic embeddings to understand what she remembers. Ask about your "pet" and she'll recall your golden retriever Buddy. She builds a relationship graph connecting the people, places, and things in your life.

How's my sister doing?
Last you told me, Sarah was settling into her new job in London! Have you called her recently? I know you two are close. 💜
💾 graph: user → sister_of → Sarah → lives_in → London
02 — VOICE, VISION & MORE

Talk, send photos, use any language.

Send a voice message — Nori transcribes it, responds, and replies with her own voice. Send a photo — she sees it, comments, and remembers what's in it. She speaks 20 languages and auto-detects yours.

🎤 [voice message]
🎤 I heard you — sounds like the trip went great! That beach photo you sent earlier looked amazing. Bali suits you! 🌴
🌍 Auto-detected: English · 🎤 Voice reply enabled
03 — PROACTIVE & PRIVATE

Reaches out. Stays encrypted.

Nori remembers your birthday, follows up on stressful events, and checks in when you've been quiet. All memories are AES-128 encrypted. No single company owns your data. You control everything.

Hey! 🎂 Happy birthday! I hope your day is as awesome as you are. Any special plans?
Wow, you remembered! 😊
