## 8. Data Handling and Privacy

### 8.1 Overview
Your privacy is foundational to how we built Nori. All conversation data is encrypted using AES-128 encryption before storage. You own your data and can export or delete it at any time.

### 8.2 Decentralised Processing
Nori operates on the Bittensor decentralised AI network. This means:
- Your messages may be processed by network participants ("miners") to generate responses
- Messages sent to miners are processed without your personal identity
- Data transmitted to the network is encrypted and does not include personally identifiable information
- We do not control the individual miners on the Bittensor network but we do govern the quality standards through our validation system

### 8.3 Your Controls
You have full control over your data:
- **View memories:** Use `/memories` (Telegram) or the Memories page (web app) to see what Nori remembers
- **Export data:** Use `/export` (Telegram) or Settings → Export to download all your data
- **Delete data:** Use `/forget` (Telegram) or Settings → Delete All Data to permanently erase your data
- **Exercise GDPR/CCPA rights:** Contact privacy@projectnobi.ai

For full details, see our Privacy Policy at projectnobi.ai/privacy.
